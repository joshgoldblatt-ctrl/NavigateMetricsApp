📖 Engagement Category Definitions

How engagement is categorized:
Engagement comes from three sources: Appointments, Events, and Cases (LaGuardia Cares). Each is evaluated based on its Care Unit and (for appointments) Location values. A student counts in a category if they have at least one qualifying appointment, event, or case.

✅ Advising Engagement

Appointment Rule: Care Unit = "Advising" AND Location is one of the approved locations
  • ASAP
  • College Discovery
  • CUNY Start
  • Health Sciences
  • International Student Services
  • Math, Engineering & Computer Science
  • Peer Advisor Academy
  • Student Advising Services
  • Veterans Services
Event Rule: Care Unit = "Advising" (case-insensitive)

💼 Career Services Engagement

Appointment Rule: Care Unit = "Career Services"

Events do not currently contribute to this category

📖 Tutoring Engagement

Appointment Rule: Care Unit = "Tutoring & Technology" AND Location is one of the approved locations
  • Academic ESL Lab
  • Academic Peer Instruction Tutoring
  • Accounting Lab
  • Anatomy & Physiology Study Hall
  • ASAP Tutoring
  • College Discovery
  • Computer Science Lab
  • Engineering Lab
  • Math Tutoring Center
  • Modern Language Lab
  • Reading Lab
  • Science Study Center
  • Student Government Association Tutoring
  • Writing Center

Events do not currently contribute to this category

🏫 Campus Life Engagement

Appointment Rules (ANY of these):
  • Option 1: Care Unit = "Campus Life"
  • Option 2: Location = "CUNY Edge" (regardless of Care Unit)
  • Option 3: Care Unit = "LaGuardia Cares" (regardless of Location)
  • Option 4: Location = "VP of Student Affairs" (regardless of Care Unit)
⚠️ Exclusion: Location = "Athletics & Recreation" is excluded from Campus Life (counts in "Any" only)
Event Rule: Care Unit = "Campus Life" (case-insensitive)

📋 Enrollment Engagement

Appointment Rule: Care Unit = "Enrollment"

Location does not matter for this category. Events do not currently contribute.

💰 Paying for College Engagement

Appointment Rule: Care Unit = "Paying for College"

Location does not matter for this category. Events do not currently contribute.

📚 Academic Departments & Library Engagement

Appointment Rule: Care Unit = "Academic Departments & Library" OR "Physical Therapist Assistant Program"
Event Rule: Care Unit = "Academic Departments & Library" OR "Physical Therapist Assistant Program" (case-insensitive)

🔄 Transfer Support Engagement

Appointment Rule: Location = "Transfer Support" (any Care Unit)
Event Rule: Care Unit = "Transfer" OR "Transfer Support (TEI)" (case-insensitive)

ℹ️ Important Notes:

  • "Any Engagement" counts all appointments AND events from enrolled students, regardless of category
  • Appointments/events that don't meet any category criteria still count toward "Any Engagement" but not specific categories
  • The "Show Uncategorized Appointments" button helps identify appointments not falling into defined categories
  • Filters apply to both the source data and the categorization results
  • Event categorization is case-insensitive (e.g., "ADVISING" and "advising" are treated the same)
