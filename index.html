📖 Care Unit Categorization

How engagement is categorized: Each record is assigned to a category equal to its raw Care Unit value, exactly as it appears in the uploaded Navigate files. There is no location-based logic, no combining of Care Units, and no renaming. A student counts toward a Care Unit if they have at least one appointment, check-in, event, or CARES case in that Care Unit. "Any Engagement" counts a student who has at least one record in any Care Unit.

🗂️ Canonical Care Units

The app recognizes the following fixed list of Care Units (alphabetical). Each gets its own score card, table column/row, and export flag:

  • Academic Departments
  • Advising
  • Athletics and Recreation
  • Career Services
  • Enrollment
  • First Year Seminar, ePortfolio and Technology
  • Paying for College
  • Peer Advisor Academy
  • Student Affairs
  • Transfer
  • Tutoring

➕ "Other" & "Any Engagement"

Other: Any record whose Care Unit is not on the canonical list above is grouped under "Other." The "Other" card and column only appear when such records exist, and they are listed in the diagnostic modal ("Show Uncategorized Appointments") so you can spot new or misspelled Care Units.
Any Engagement: Counts every enrolled student with at least one record in any Care Unit — the top-line reach number.

ℹ️ Important Notes

  • Categorization uses the raw Care Unit value only — Location is preserved in exports but no longer affects which category a record falls into.
  • Two metrics are available per Care Unit: enrolled reach (unique enrolled students with ≥1 record) and total volume (count of all records, enrolled or not, shown in the Contributions table).
  • The Source column is auto-generated: "Appointment", "Check-in", or "CARES Case".
  • Tableau Exports: The Engagement Records and Student Roster exports share Student ID + Semester. The "Assigned Category" column has been removed from the Engagement Records — use the Care Unit column directly. The Student Roster includes a Had [Care Unit] Engagement flag for each canonical Care Unit, plus Any and Other.
